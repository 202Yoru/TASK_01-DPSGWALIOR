Flowers

Rose

Roses are of many colours, mainly red, yellow.
Rose flower is viewed as a symbol of balance.
Rose has an attractive and a pleasant fragrance.
The rose flower is famous all over the world for its beauty.
Rose is a common plant and is found in almost every garden.

Sunflower

Sunflower is a very famous plant in the world.
It is known for bright yellow color flowers that always face the sun.
The sunflowers are cultivated in open fields in large numbers.
Sunflowers are grown up as they are used to make vegetable oil.
